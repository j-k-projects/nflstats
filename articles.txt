//How do I create the videos I post on twitter?
1. Buy bandicam ($40) -- https://www.bandicam.com/
2. Record screen with bandicam, which saves file on your computer
3. Upload created file to streamable
4. Crop on streamable as necessary
5. Download file from streamable (open video, right click on it, press download) and post to twitter
