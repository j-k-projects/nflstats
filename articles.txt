//How do I create the videos I post on twitter?
1. Buy bandicam ($40) -- https://www.bandicam.com/
2. Record screen with bandicam, which saves file on your computer. (in Video --> Format --> Settings, make sure "File Type" is .MP4)
3. Upload to twitter
